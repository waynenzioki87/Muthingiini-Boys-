Our Community Partnerships

Muthiingini Boys High School is proud to be part of the local community. We collaborate with parents, alumni and local organizations to support student success and community development.

Parent-Teacher Association

Our PTA works closely with the school to enhance student welfare, organize events and provide resources for academic excellence.

Alumni Network

Graduates stay connected through our alumni association, offering mentorship, career guidance and support for current students.

Local Partnerships

We partner with local businesses and NGOs for internships, community service projects and extracurricular opportunities.

Environmental Initiatives

Students participate in tree planting, clean-up drives and conservation projects to protect our local environment.
