Motto Explained

“Nihil Praeter Optimum”

The school motto means “Nothing But The Best.” It is more than a slogan — it is the standard that guides teaching, learning and the behaviour of every member of our community.

We expect our students to give their best effort in the classroom, on the pitch, in the dormitory, and in service to others.

— A core value of Muthiingini Boys High School

When our boys embrace this motto, they become scholars who work hard, athletes who compete with integrity, and citizens who lead with humility.
