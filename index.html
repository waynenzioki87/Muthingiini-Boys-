About Our School

Shaping Leaders of Tomorrow

Muthiingini Boys High School is a premier national boys' school located in Kiambu County, Kenya. Since our founding, we have been dedicated to academic excellence, moral integrity, and the holistic development of every student.

Our motto — Nihil Praeter Optimum — is not merely a phrase. It is a living commitment upheld by every student, teacher, and alumnus. We pursue nothing but the best in academics, sports, arts, and character.

History & Mission

Established over six decades ago, we have grown from a humble boarding school into one of Kenya's respected national institutions.

Vision & Values

We nurture disciplined leaders who are academically strong, socially responsible, and guided by integrity.

School Leadership

Our leadership team combines experience, compassion and a commitment to maintaining a safe and inspiring learning environment.

Motto Explained

“Nothing But The Best” means every student strives for excellence while showing respect, loyalty and moral strength.
