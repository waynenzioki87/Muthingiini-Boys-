Muthiingini Boys High School

History & Mission

Our Legacy, Our Purpose

Muthiingini Boys High School was established to provide young Kenyan men with a strong foundation in academic excellence, personal discipline, and civic responsibility. Over the years, the school has grown into a nationally respected institution known for outstanding exam performance and well-rounded leadership development.

Our History

Founded in 1963, Muthiingini Boys began with a small student body and a dedicated staff committed to shaping the future of the nation. The school has evolved steadily, adding modern facilities and expanding academic programs while maintaining its core values.

Our Mission

We exist to empower every student to achieve their full potential through rigorous academics, strong character formation, and opportunities for leadership. We foster an environment where respect, responsibility, and resilience are practiced every day.
