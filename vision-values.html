Vision & Values

Our Vision for Every Student

We aim to build a school where every boy becomes an excellent student, a principled leader, and a caring member of society. Our vision is to be the preferred national boys school for families seeking rigorous academics and strong character formation.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in scholarship, sports, arts and student conduct. Excellence is the benchmark for learning and achievement.

Integrity

Honesty and upright behaviour are at the heart of our community. Every student is encouraged to act responsibly and with honour.

Respect

We value mutual respect between staff, students and families. Our school culture is built on kindness, discipline and teamwork.

Leadership

Students learn to lead by example, serve others, and make positive decisions both inside and outside the classroom.
