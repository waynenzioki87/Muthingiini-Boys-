School Leadership

Leadership Committed to Excellence

The leadership team blends experience, compassion and a strong focus on academic and personal growth. Every decision is guided by our mission to provide students with a safe, disciplined and inspiring learning environment.

Principal

The principal oversees curriculum standards, staff development, and the school’s long-term vision to ensure excellence in every area.

Deputy Principal

The deputy principal manages student welfare, discipline and day-to-day operations, ensuring the school remains organized and supportive.

Board of Governors

The board provides strategic guidance, governance oversight and community accountability for the school’s performance.

Heads of Departments

Each department head supports teachers, improves instructional quality and promotes subject-specific achievement across the school.
